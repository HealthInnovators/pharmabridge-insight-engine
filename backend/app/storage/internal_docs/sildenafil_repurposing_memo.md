# Sildenafil repurposing notes (demo)

This internal note summarizes hypotheses for sildenafil repurposing.

## Rationale
- PDE5 inhibition affects NO/cGMP signaling.
- Potential neurovascular and inflammatory modulation mechanisms could be relevant for neuropathic pain.

## Evidence gaps
- Limited clinical evidence for neuropathic pain.
- Need to validate dosing, duration, and safety in chronic use.

## Risks
- Hypotension and contraindications with nitrates.
- Drug-drug interactions.
